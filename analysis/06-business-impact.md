## 2. Revenue Enablement

### Current challenges
- Slow expansion into new markets  
- Limited ability to launch new payment features quickly  

### Improvements
- Modular architecture enables faster product launches  
- API ecosystem supports partner integrations  
- Scalable platform supports global growth  

### Expected impact
- Faster time-to-market for new payment solutions  
- Increased merchant acquisition

---

## 3. Risk Reduction

### Current challenges
- Fraud detection complexity  
- Real-time risk assessment latency  
- Regulatory requirements across markets  

### Improvements
- Dedicated risk and fraud services  
- Real-time event-driven monitoring  
- Centralized compliance capabilities  

### Expected impact
- Reduced fraud losses  
- Improved credit decision accuracy  
- Stronger regulatory compliance  

---

## 4. Scalability & Performance

### Current challenges
- High transaction volumes strain systems  
- Performance bottlenecks in monolithic components  

### Improvements
- Microservices architecture enables horizontal scaling  
- Event-driven design reduces system dependencies  
- Cloud-native infrastructure improves elasticity  

### Expected impact
- Improved system reliability  
- Better performance during peak traffic  
- Ability to support global growth  

---

## 5. Customer & Merchant Experience

### Improvements
- Faster checkout experience  
- Improved payment success rates  
- Better onboarding experience for merchants  

### Expected impact
- Higher conversion rates  
- Increased customer satisfaction  
- Stronger merchant retention  

---

## Summary

The proposed architecture enables Klarna to:

- Scale globally with reduced complexity  
- Launch new products faster  
- Improve operational efficiency  
- Strengthen risk management  
- Deliver better customer and merchant experiences
